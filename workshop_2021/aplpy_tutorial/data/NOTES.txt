== Notes ==
The YSO catalog came from the following sources obtained through the Aladin software:

Catalog of Star-Forming Regions in the Galaxy (Avedisova, 2002)
Catalogue of bright YSO candidates in ISOGAL (Felli+, 2002)
A database of dense cores mapped in ammonia (Jijina+, 1999)
Ammonia on YSOs IRAS sources (Molinari+ 1996)
Catalogue of massive young stellar objects (Chan+, 1996)
